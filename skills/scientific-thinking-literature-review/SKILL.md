# Literature Review

Use this skill when the task is to find, screen, synthesize, and cite a body of academic or technical literature.

## When to Use

- Building a systematic, scoping, or narrative literature review.
- Synthesizing the state of the art for a research question.
- Finding gaps or contradictions.
- Preparing citation-backed background sections.

## Review Types

- **Narrative review**: broad synthesis for orientation.
- **Scoping review**: maps concepts, methods, and evidence gaps.
- **Systematic review**: predefined protocol, reproducible search, explicit screening and exclusion.
- **Meta-analysis**: systematic review plus quantitative effect aggregation.

Default to scoping for exploratory work and systematic for publication or clinical claims.

## Workflow

1. **Define the question.** Use PICO for clinical work or system/method/comparison/metric for technical work.
2. **Plan the search.** Choose databases, date range, languages, types, inclusion/exclusion criteria, and exact search strings.
3. **Search and log.** Keep a reproducible log: database, date, query, filters, results, export.
4. **Deduplicate.** Order: DOI, PMID/arXiv ID, exact title, normalized title + first author + year.
5. **Screen.** Stages: title, abstract.
6. **Extract data.** Use a structured table: Study, Design, Population/Data, Method, Comparator, Outcome, Key finding, Limitations.
7. **Synthesize.** Group by theme. Separate claims by confidence: high, medium, low.
8. **Verify citations.** Confirm identifiers; do not cite a paper for a claim it does not make; label preprints and reviews.

See [references/literature-review-template.md](references/literature-review-template.md) for templates and examples.

## Pitfalls

- Do not treat search snippets as evidence.
- Do not mix preprints, reviews, and primary studies without labeling them.
- Do not omit negative or conflicting findings.
- Do not claim systematic-review rigor without a reproducible protocol.
- Do not use a single database for a broad claim unless scope is explicitly limited.
